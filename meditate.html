Begin Your Meditation Journey

Take a moment to breathe, find your center, and begin your path to mindfulness

Sarah Chen

Meditation Guide & Mindfulness Coach

1

Find Your Space

Choose a quiet, comfortable place where you won't be disturbed. Sit or lie down in a position that feels natural to you.

2

Set Your Intention

Take a moment to reflect on why you're meditating today. What do you hope to achieve or experience?

3

Focus on Your Breath

Notice the natural rhythm of your breath. Feel the air moving in and out of your body.

4

Stay Present

When your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back to your breath. This is normal and part of the practice.

Pro Tips

  • •Start with shorter sessions and gradually increase the duration
  • •Practice at the same time each day to build a consistent habit
  • •Don't judge your thoughts - simply observe them and let them pass
  • •Use ambient sounds to help maintain focus and create a peaceful atmosphere
